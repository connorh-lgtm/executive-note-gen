# Macy's

## Overview
- **Industry:** Retail / Department Stores
- **Status:** Developing to Scaling — AI as digital growth lever and supply chain modernization tool

## Situation
- **Focus:** AI-driven shopping assistance, hyper-personalization, and supply chain modernization with AI + robotics in new fulfillment infrastructure
- **Recent Activity:** China Grove automated fulfillment center opened with robotics + AI (2025); capex $740M mainly driven by digital/technology; experimenting with AI in merchandising/planning/marketing

### Challenges
- Replacing existing systems, some of which are older legacy systems
- Outsourcing processes and cloud migration with implementation risks (data loss/corruption, disruptions)
- Cost overruns and operational disruption risk from modernization

## Key Initiatives
- AI-driven digital experience: intelligent shopping assistance, agentic commerce, hyper-personalized experiences
- China Grove automated fulfillment center (2025): state-of-the-art with automation, robotics, and AI
- AI experimentation across merchandising, planning, and marketing for inventory/demand optimization

## Positioning
- **Focus:** Supply-chain AI + automation integration tied to cost-to-serve KPIs; cloud migration + data governance programs with strong cutover assurance
- **Competitive:** Automation/robotics vendors for fulfillment; cloud migration partners

### Differentiators
- State-of-the-art fulfillment with robotics + AI (China Grove center)
- Explicit investment in agentic commerce and hyper-personalization
- Heavy capex commitment to digital/technology ($740M in 2025, ~$800M guided for 2026)
